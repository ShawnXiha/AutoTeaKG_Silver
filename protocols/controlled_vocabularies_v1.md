## 10. Adjudication Principles Added After Mini Gold Set v1

1. Review-derived interpretation papers can use `mixed` effect direction when they do not assert a simple positive efficacy claim.
2. Do not preserve specific taxa names in the gold set unless verified from abstract/full text.
3. Human meta-analyses spanning heterogeneous interventions should default to conservative endpoint wording.
4. Phase-2 context papers may be retained in the corpus but excluded from phase-1 core efficacy summaries.
