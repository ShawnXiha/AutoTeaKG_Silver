# Internal Peer Review of v1 Draft

Date: 2026-04-12

## Overall Assessment

The v1 draft is a coherent first manuscript version. The main story is clear: AutoTeaKG-Silver is an automated, provenance-rich, uncertainty-aware evidence graph for tea functional activity literature. The Results section is supported by generated KG v3 query tables and publication-style figures.

## Strengths

- The paper no longer depends on manual annotation as the main contribution.
- The uncertainty-aware framing is credible and appropriately underclaims the resource as silver-standard.
- The processing/extraction context experiment provides a concrete methodological narrative rather than a flat database description.
- Figures are already integrated and compiled into the PDF.

## Major Issues To Fix Before Submission

1. Related Work is missing as a standalone section.
2. Methods should include a more explicit reproducibility table listing input files, scripts, and output directories.
3. The current paper is written in generic article format, not a target journal or conference template.
4. Some result claims should be supported by small inline tables in addition to figures.
5. Citation coverage is adequate for v1 but should be expanded for knowledge graph construction, biomedical text mining, and food bioactivity databases.

## Minor Issues

- One small overfull hbox remains in the compiled log; it does not block readability.
- Figure placement is acceptable for v1 but should be tuned after choosing a venue template.
- The Discussion could include a sharper paragraph on why full-text availability becomes the bottleneck.

## Recommendation

Proceed to v2 by adding Related Work, reproducibility details, and a compact table of pipeline-stage statistics. The current draft is suitable as a working manuscript foundation.
