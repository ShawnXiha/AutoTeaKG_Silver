Trigger: Idea tournament completed for tea functional activity database / KG direction

## What Changed

Added three feasible directions to ideation memory:
- MI-001 Evidence-graded tea functional activity knowledge base
- MI-002 Tea microbiome mechanism graph

## Why

The tournament showed a consistent pattern:
- Directions with immediate structured value and manageable scope outperformed broad all-at-once KG designs.
- Mechanism-rich microbiome work ranked highly but is better treated as a focused graph extension rather than the first product.
- Predictive and copilot ideas underperformed because they depend on a reliable curated substrate.

## Expected Impact on Future Cycles

1. New tea-domain tournaments should seed from MI-001, MI-002, and MI-003 instead of restarting from a generic “tea KG” idea.
2. Broad graph proposals should be checked against the “scope first, graph later” principle before expansion.
3. Predictive modeling branches should not be treated as phase-1 candidates unless a curated evidence layer already exists.
